5 # LIS302DL MEMS MOTION SENSOR
6 
7 ## Brief
8 
9 The LIS302DL is an ultra compact low-power three axes linear accelerometer producted by [ST](www.st.com). It
10 includes a sensing element and an IC interface able to provide the measured
11 acceleration to the external world through I2C/SPI serial interface.
12 
13 The sensing element, capable of detecting the acceleration, is manufactured
14 using a dedicated process developed by ST to produce inertial sensors and
15 actuators in silicon.
16 
17 The IC interface is manufactured using a CMOS process that allows to design a
18 dedicated circuit which is trimmed to better match the sensing element
19 characteristics.
20 
21 The LIS302DL has dynamically user selectable full scales of ±2g/8g and it is
22 capable of measuring accelerations with an output data rate of 100Hz or 400Hz.
23 
24 A self-test capability allows the user to check the functioning of the sensor
25 in the final application.
26 
27 The device may be configured to generate inertial wake-up/free-fall interrupt signals when a programmable acceleration threshold is crossed at least in one of the three axes. Thresholds and timing of interrupt generators are programmable by the end user on the fly.
28 
29 The LIS302DL is available in plastic Thin Land Grid Array package (TLGA) and it is guaranteed to operate over an extended temperature range from -40℃ to +85℃.
30 
31 ## Key Features
32 
33 * 2.16V to 3.6V supply voltage
34 * 1.8V compatible IOs
35 * &lt;W power consumption
36 * +/- 2g / +/- 8g dynamically selectable full-scale
37 * I2C/SPI digital output interface
38 * Programmable multiple interrupt generator
39 * Click and double click recognition
40 * Embedded high pass filter
41 * Embedded self test
42 * 10000g high shock survivability
43 * ECOPACK RoHS and "Green" compliant

102  //

111  printf("%s\r\n", WelcomeInfo);
